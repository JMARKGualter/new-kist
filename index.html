Ventures Hub was inspired by a clear mission: to create a space that moves at the speed of innovation.

Application and Approval

STEP 1

Initial Engagement & Company Introduction

Action Items:
  • a. Introduce the company and coordinate a facility tour.
STEP 2

Invitation for Site Facility Tour

Action Items:
  • a. During the visit, confirm the potential space allocation.
  • b. Discuss prospective collaboration opportunities and the shared benefits of locating at BatStateU KIST Park.
STEP 3

Initial Submission of Required Documents

Objective: To formalize the intention to locate, the interested locator submits:

Action Items:
  • a. Letter of Intent (indicating space preference).
  • b. Planned Business Activities at KIST Park (e.g., R&D, administrative operations, mass production, etc.), including the planned equipment and machinery to be installed within the premises.
  • c. Proposed Engagement with the University
STEP 4

Review and Issuance of Certification as Eligible Industry Locator

Action Items:
  • a. The Facility Manager reviews all submitted documents to assess the company’s eligibility based on BatStateU’s evaluation criteria. The appropriate pricing will be determined by BatStateU, taking into account the company’s level of engagement.
  • b. Following a favorable evaluation, KIST Park issues an Acceptance Letter with Notice to Proceed for SEC and BIR Registration through a formal letter.
  • c. The Certificate of Eligibility shall be issued once SEC registration is completed.
  • d. The locator may begin working on other PEZA requirements.
STEP 5

Preparation and Signing of the Memorandum of Agreement (MOA)

Action Items:
  • a. Draft and notarize the MOA (including the agreed lease start date, to be duly communicated to PEZA).
  • b. Attach the following documents:
    i.SEC Registration and Updated Articles of Incorporation;
    ii. BIR FORM 2303;
    iii.Secretary's Certificate to duly-authorized representative/s (if applicable)
    iv. Business Permit/ Mayors Permit(if applicable)
    v. Existing PEZA Registration (if applicable).
  • Note: The notarized MOA forms part of the PEZA registration requirements.
STEP 6

Finalization and Operationalization

Action Items:
  • a. The locator shall complete the submission of all PEZA documentary requirements and comply with all applicable conditions to secure PEZA registration.
  • b. The space shall be handed over to the locator for fit-out and operational setup.
  • c. Rental shall commence based on the agreed lease start date.
STEP 7

Monitoring and Evaluation

Action Items:
  • a. Conduct regular meetings.
  • b. Evaluate the impact of the industry locator’s presence.
  • c. Address issues promptly.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What Is KIST Park?

In general, KIST Park was initiated by the Philippine Economic Zone Authority (PEZA) as a new form of special economic zones that will allow state universities and colleges (SUCs) to maximize large areas of idle land in their landholdings.

2. How was BatStateU KIST Park established as a PEZA-registered Special Economic Zone?

On May 22, 2020, former President Rodrigo Roa Duterte signed Proclamation No. 976, officially designating a parcel of land within Batangas State University (BatStateU) as a Special Economic Zone, to be known as the BatStateU Knowledge, Innovation, and Science Technology (KIST) Park. With this designation, BatStateU KIST Park became a PEZA-registered economic zone, opening opportunities for businesses, industry locators, and research institutions to operate within a globally competitive innovation ecosystem while benefiting from investment incentives and fostering technology commercialization and industry-academe collaboration.

3. What is BatStateU KIST Park in a nutshell?

Batangas State University KIST Park is a special economic zone located in Batangas City, Philippines offering space for technology-based industry locators and providing access to top-tier talent and tailored innovation resources.

4. Is BatStateU KIST Park currently accepting locators?

BatStateU KIST Park is now welcoming industry locators at the Ventures Hub. Available are 18 and 36 sqm units.

Plus! Reservations are officially open for future occupancy in the Design Innovation Building with 145sqm, 237sqm and 261sqm floor area.

Interested locators may also request a guided site tour—just let us know ahead of time so we can arrange it properly.

5. What types of locators does BatStateU KIST Park accommodate?

BatStateU KIST Park primarily welcomes tech-based startup companies and R&D units, seeking not only locator spaces but also a strong innovation ecosystem to support their R&D and business development.

6. What are the perks of being a BatStateU KIST Park locator?

a) Enjoy PEZA Incentives – Access tax benefits, duty-free importation, and other government incentives exclusive to PEZA-registered enterprises.
b) Access to Innovation Resources – Utilize BatStateU’s cutting-edge R&D facilities and top-tier talent for research and product development.
c) Benefit from in-house programs, including the following Exclusive Startup Incubation Support:
Business development services
Mentorship and coaching
Networking and industry linkages
Legal and intellectual property support
Plus, even more opportunities!

7. Do you have pricing for industry locators?

Yes, pricing can be discussed further along with potential engagement activities.

8. Are engagement activities required?

Absolutely! BatStateU KIST Park is more than just a space—it’s built on collaboration and innovation, promoting industry-academe collaboration.

9. What is the process for industry locators to set up at BatStateU KIST Park?

It’s a simple three-step process:
a) Inquire – Share your company’s vision and explore opportunities through a guided site tripping.
b) Explore Synergy – Discuss potential collaborations and ensure alignment for mutual success.
c) Submit Requirements & Secure Your Spot – Once all plans and requirements are finalized, you can sign the agreement and officially secure your space.

10. What are the requirements to be eligible as a KIST Locator?

a) Letter of Intent to locate, including space preference
b) Company and Business Operations Overview
c) Business Registration
d) Certificate of Registration
e) Articles of Incorporation
f) Proposed programs and activities
g) Secretary’s Certificate for duly-authorized representative/s (if applicable)
h) Biodata of Principal Officers
i) PEZA Registration (BatStateU KIST Park can assist companies to register in PEZA)
j) Business Permit (if applicable)
k) Audited Financial Statements (if applicable)
l) Other documents as may be prescribed by BatStateU KIST Park

11. Are industry locators required to be PEZA-Registered?

Yes, since BatStateU KIST Park is a special economic zone, all industry locators must be PEZA-registered to qualify for incentives and fully operate within the zone. For more information visit https://www.peza.gov.ph/.

12. What are the business classifications under PEZA?

a.) Domestic Market Enterprise (DME) – Businesses that primarily sell goods or services within the Philippines and cater to the local market.
b.) Export-Oriented Enterprise (EOE) – Businesses that export at least 70% of their total production to foreign markets.
