Verde Island Passage Center for Oceanographic Research and Aquatic Life Science(VIPCORALS)
Facebook Page: https://www.facebook.com/vipcorals.batstateu
Mandate VIP CORALS envisions to be the pioneer, leading, proactive, and world-class research center in the field of oceanographic research and aquatic life sciences in the Verde Island Passage.
Service Description
Training on assessment methods for different marine ecosystems Providing hands-on training on methodologies for assessing marine ecosystems, including biodiversity monitoring and environmental impact studies.
Training on aquaculture/sea ranching of different marine organisms Conducting training programs on sustainable aquaculture and sea ranching techniques for marine species to support conservation and livelihood initiatives.
Training on current methods in marine research Offering capacity-building sessions on the latest research techniques in marine science, including taxonomy, mapping, and exploration of marine species.
