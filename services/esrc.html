Mandate The Electronic Systems Research Center (ESRC) is committed to providing technical support for research and development related to the field of electronics.
SERVICES
Electronic prototyping Development of functional electronic models to test and refine concepts before final production.
Measurement and Data logging Recording and analyzing data from experiments or real-world applications for research and development.
Schematic design Creating circuit diagrams to visualize and plan electronic systems.
Simulation Using software tools to test and optimize electronic circuits or systems before physical implementation.
PCB Design Designing Printed Circuit Boards (PCBs) to integrate electronic components efficiently.
Custom Experimental Setup Developing specialized setups for research experiments based on specific project requirements.
Modeling using Computer Aided Design Creating digital models of components, systems, or structures for simulation and prototyping.
3D Printing Fabricating prototypes or parts using additive manufacturing for rapid testing and development.
Training Providing hands-on learning sessions on various technical and research-related topics such as Arduino Training, PCB Fabrication, WebDesign, Latex for Research, EasyEDA, etc.
Research Consultation Offering expert guidance on research methodologies, experimental design, and technical problem-solving.
*Pricing structure is yet to be released. Majority of the services are provided to students free of charge.
