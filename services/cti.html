Startup Incubation Services
Business Development CTI shall work with incubatee to create a well-defined business plan or business model and marketing strategy, based on extensive market research, competitive analysis, and incubatee's specific objectives.
Business Operation Services The incubatee shall be provided office space equipped with a telephone, computer, printer, office supplies, wi-fi, conference room, and co-working space located at the CTI Office, 2/F STEER Hub Building, Batangas State University Alangilan Campus.
Legal Services CTI shall assist the incubatee in any business permits, and intellectual property protection and ensure that the incubatee is in regulatory compliance.
Marketing Services CTI shall provide marketing services that will help incubatee to promote its product and/or reach its target market in the most cost-efficient ways.
A. Send to participate in trade shows and other exposure events
B. Public Relations / Press Releases
C. Print Collateral - Brochure/Catalogs
Training Services Incubatee may receive business and technical consulting services from local business professionals and/or faculty members from Batangas State University, and others (collectively, "CTI Resident Mentors"). Resident Mentorship may entail one-on-one work between incubatee and Resident Mentor.
Access to Technical Support Through BatStateU The NEU CTI, incubatee shall gain access to the technical and scientific resources of BatStateU The NEU which are shared laboratories, equipment, and research facilities under the supervision of the authorized person in charge.
Access to Funding Support Through BatStateU The NEU CTI, incubatee may request R&D support from the institutional funds subject to existing budgeting, accounting, and auditing rules and regulation. Each eligible startup may propose from Php 50,000.00 to Php 250,000.00 R&D support. In addition, incubatee shall also get linked to external partners from the government, industry, venture capitalists, and investors for funding support or raising capital.
* Startup incubation is offered free of charge. Structured in batches, only available to those that meet the eligibility criteria outlined in the designated call for incubation.
