GIS APPLICATIONS DEVELOPMENT CENTER
Facebook Page: https://www.facebook.com/gis.center.bsuteu
Mandate The GADC aims to create interdisciplinary collaborations for GIS-technology solutions for research, education, and community use.
Service Description Ideal for
Spatial Data Processing & Analysis Capturing, storing, checking, processing, and displaying spatial and non-spatial data for research and projects. Researchers, urban planners, environmentalists, government agencies, businesses needing location-based insights.
Drone Imagery & Aerial Photography Collecting high-resolution imagery for mapping, environmental monitoring, and land assessment. Agricultural studies, disaster response, infrastructure planning, land-use analysis.
GIS Training & Seminars Conducting workshops, seminars, and hands-on training to enhance GIS skills and knowledge. Students, faculty, researchers, government agencies, private sectors requiring GIS proficiency.
GIS Consultation Expert advice on GIS applications for project and study assessment. Individuals, research groups, startups, or industries exploring GIS-based solutions.
Map Layout & Creation Generating customized maps using acquired or provided data, including map printing and image interpretation. Local government units, businesses, environmental projects, infrastructure planning, research studies.
GADC follows a tailored partnership approach, offering customized solutions instead of a fixed list of services. Driven by research, it prioritizes long-term collaborations, empowering organizations in their GIS-driven initiatives
