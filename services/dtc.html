DIGITAL TRANSFORMATION CENTER
Facebook Page: https://www.facebook.com/dtc.batstateu
Mandate The Digital Transformation Center's prime objective is to be at the forefront of ICT innovations in the use of Internet-of-Things (IoT), Data Science, and many other emerging ICT facets that benefit the community.
SERVICES
Training and Mentorship - Conceptual and technical training on digital transformation
- Consulting services from research center heads and Batangas State University faculty
Technical Assistance - Access to shared laboratories, research equipment, and facilities
- Support from technical and scientific experts
Funding Assistance - Eligibility to request institutional funding
- Assistance in securing funding from external government and industry partners
Project Implementation Support - Guidance in implementing digital transformation initiatives
- Assistance in integrating digital systems and processes
Research Collaboration and Resource Sharing - Opportunities to collaborate with experts and stakeholders
- Access to digital transformation research insights and best practices
*DTC operates on a tailored partnership model rather than providing a fixed list of services to the general public.
