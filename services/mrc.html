Mandate The Manufacturing Research Center serves as a technology access center, training provider, service bureau and collaboration hub for innovations, research and development (R&D), and support services.
Types of Services
Service Description Cost of Services
1. Digital Design Support/ Assistance FAB LAB will conduct support and assistance with digital designing, giving him/her the utmost attention in providing guidance. Php 2,000
2. Digital Designing FAB LAB will conduct digital designing based on the requirements of the client. Php 4,000
3. Computer Numerically Controlled Router Machine FAB LAB provides clients with the use of the CNC router machine to cater to their woodworking needs. Php 90 per hour
4. Laser Engraver / Cutter FAB LAB provides clients with the equipment of Laser Cutter for Engraving, Etching, and Cutting. (Minimum of 5 minutes) Php 20 per minute
5. Vinyl Plotter/Cutter FAB LAB provides clients the permission to use the equipment vinyl plotter for High-Speed Specialized Printing. Php 25 per square foot
6. Large Format Printer FAB LAB provides the large-format printing services which print into different materials such as vinyl stickers, tarpaulins, etc. Php 32.5 per square foot
7. Vacuum Forming FAB LAB provides clients with the usage of a vacuum forming machine to be used in packaging or other products provided by the client. Php 20 per hour
8. Computer Numerically Controlled Milling Machine FAB LAB provides clients with the use of the CNC router machine to cater to their precision cutting needs. Php 90 per hour
9. 3D Printer FAB LAB provides clients to use 3D printers for their prototyping needs. Fused-Deposition Modelling: Php 1 per minute
Composite 3D Printing & Stereolithography: Php 5 per minute
