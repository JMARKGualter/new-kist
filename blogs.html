What’s Ahead for BatStateU KIST Park in 2025: Modular Spaces and Opportunities for Locators

Batangas State University’s Knowledge, Innovation, and Science Technology (KIST) Park is entering an exciting phase in 2025. To provide an immediate and innovative workspace solution, Dr. Tirso A. Ronquillo, Chief Operating Officer, has introduced Ventures Hub—33 modular locator spaces built with industry-grade container vans.

Ventures Hub was designed with a clear mission: to move at the speed of innovation. Recognizing the need for agile, scalable, and future-ready workspaces, BatStateU KIST Park embraced a modular approach that allows startups and industry locators to launch, build, and grow their ventures without delay. More than just an interim space, Ventures Hub serves as a high-impact launchpad, providing an environment where businesses can set up quickly, customize their spaces, and expand as they scale.

KIST Park is now inviting industry locators, startups, and innovation-driven companies to reserve their space at Ventures Hub and be among the first to establish operations in this dynamic environment. Early access provides businesses with a cost-effective, scalable, and collaborative workspace—empowering them to lead in shaping the future of their industries. At BatStateU KIST Park, locators aren’t just tenants; they are part of an evolving ecosystem where ventures don’t just start—they thrive.
