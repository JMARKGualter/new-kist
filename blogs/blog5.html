FusionNode Launches Cybersecurity Training Program at BatStateU KIST Park

September 26, 2024
Audio Visual Room, STEER Hub Bldg. BatStateU Alangilan Campus

FusionNode has hit the ground running at Batangas State University KIST Park with the launch of an innovative cybersecurity training program designed to arm graduates and faculty members with cutting-edge skills. In an era where cyber threats are evolving rapidly, this initiative couldn’t be more timely. As part of Batangas State University’s Innovation Month Celebration, this initiative reflects the university's commitment to fostering technological advancement and real-world application.

Batangas State University, as the first PEZA-registered Knowledge, Innovation, and Science Technology (KIST) Park in the country, continues to push the boundaries of education and industry collaboration. Through this cybersecurity program, the university is providing an invaluable platform for graduates and faculty to engage with industry leaders like FusionNode. The program, unlike is designed to deepen expertise in cybersecurity, focusing on real-world application that addresses the current demands of the tech industry. Faculty members also gain access to the latest in cyber defense strategies, which they can integrate into their curriculum, further aligning academic instruction with real-time industry needs.

FusionNode’s CEO and founder, Jerry Sto. Tomas, spearheads this initiative, reflecting a shared vision with the university to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and industry practice. The program is a step toward fortifying the nation’s cybersecurity landscape, addressing the growing need for skilled professionals in this critical field.

The university's efforts extend beyond this training. This collaborative effort between FusionNode and BatStateU aims to develop 100 interns by the end of the year, while strengthening the university's role as a catalyst for innovation and professional development.

Together, BatStateU and FusionNode are not just building a safer digital infrastructure, they are creating a robust pipeline of talent that will drive technological advancement and national progress in the years to come.
